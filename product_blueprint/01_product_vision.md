# Product Vision

## Working Definition

The future product should be defined as:

**A local-first tool for turning PDFs and topic summaries into a structured, interactive personal question bank.**

Chinese version:

**一个以本地使用为核心、可将 PDF 与题目摘要整理成互动式个人题库的工具。**

## Product Goal

The goal is to make exam materials, past papers, and self-organized summaries easier to:

- import
- structure
- browse
- practice
- track
- revise over time

## What Problem It Solves

Many learners already have the content they need, but it is hard to use effectively because:

- questions are trapped inside PDFs
- notes and statuses are scattered
- topics are not structured consistently
- users have to flip between question lists and source papers manually
- revision progress is hard to track

## Product Positioning

The product should be positioned as:

- local-first
- personal-use-first
- input-flexible
- question-bank-centered

It should not initially be positioned as:

- a cloud collaboration platform
- a public content marketplace
- a full learning management system
- an AI answer-generation platform

## Target Users

### Primary Users

- university students revising from past papers
- self-learners organizing their own study material
- exam candidates preparing with PDF-based resources

### Secondary Users

- tutors building personal teaching banks
- researchers or interview candidates organizing question sets

## Product Principle

The product should separate:

- source material
- structured question bank
- practice interfaces
- generated outputs

This prevents the system from becoming tied to one specific course or one specific workflow.
